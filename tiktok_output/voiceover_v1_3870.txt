The Cold War’s weirdest mission involved... a cat with a wire. The CIA’s plan: implant a mic in a cat's ear. To eavesdrop on two men. A transmitter in its back, a battery in its belly. Total cost: $20 million. They tested it. The cat heard them. The transmitter... did not. They sent the cat to spy on a Soviet embassy. It worked for five minutes. A taxi hit it. Killed it instantly. Mission compromised. The CIA report concluded... cats could be trained for such missions. They spent a fortune. Lost a cat. To learn something we already knew.
