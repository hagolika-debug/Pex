Boston, 1872. The city burned for 2 days. Then, the *real* horror started. The Great Boston Fire destroyed 776 buildings. It was a cataclysm. In the smoking rubble, survivors reported something unnatural. Phantom firefighters in old-fashioned gear, seen battling flames that weren't there. This wasn't one person. Hundreds reported identical spectral figures. And apparitions of those who died, silently pointing to their own graves. Psychologists have a name for this: mass hysteria. Trauma + gas leaks +恐怖的报纸. The city wasn't haunted by ghosts... it was haunted by its own grief. History's weirdest ghost story. Have you heard of it? Duet with your theory. 👇
