A silent explosion flattened an entire forest without leaving a crater. In 1908, Siberian herders watched the morning sky tear open. A blinding fireball vaporized the air and shattered every window. Eighty million trees fell like matchsticks in perfect circles. Scientists searched for decades but found only scorched earth. It was a meteor that exploded high above the ground. The sky’s invisible hammer still reminds us how fragile Earth is.
