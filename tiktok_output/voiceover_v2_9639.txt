Imagine looking up and seeing THIS fall from the sky. Bath County, Kentucky. March 3rd, 1876. A perfectly normal day. Then, a red, gristly substance began falling over a huge area. People collected it. Some cooked and ate it. They said it tasted like venison. Scientists guessed: dead birds? meteor meat? a slaughterhouse explosion? The answer is way weirder. It was vomited. By a giant flock of vultures. A massive flock got spooked mid-meal, regurgitating their feast miles away. Think of their confusion. Fear. Then... curiosity. They ate the sky-meat.
